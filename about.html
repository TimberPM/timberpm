About

Timber PM is a proposed business focused on the manufacturing, outfitting, and distribution of high-quality medical supplies and equipment for mobile healthcare vehicles, including box trucks, vans, and trailers. With the rising demand for mobile clinics in underserved areas, corporate settings, and emergency response, Timber PM aims to become the go-to provider for outfitting medical vehicles with compliant, reliable, and innovative supplies.
